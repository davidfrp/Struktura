Struktura

It is fast. Mobile-first. And works on all devices... This is Skruktura. A simple open-source template for modern websites.

Mobile-friendly

Struktura have been build entitely from scratch, and is only using CSS. One of the key elements of making Struktura, was the importance of keeping it mobile-friendly.

It is important to let the design flow and visual appearance stay same, whether you're looking at the website from either a mobile or laptop. Struktura does therefore treat mobiles with the extra loving care that they need.

Lightning fast

With less than 200 lines of CSS, Struktura excels at keeping the loading time as short as possible.
